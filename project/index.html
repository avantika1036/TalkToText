How It Works

1. Sign Up & Log In

Create your free account as a patient or a therapist to access your personalized portal.

2. Practice & Get Instant Feedback

Patients practice assigned sentences or free-form speech, receiving immediate AI-powered pronunciation analysis.

3. Doctor Reviews & Assigns

Therapists review patient progress, provide personalized feedback, and assign new exercises or customize scoring rubrics.

4. Continuous Improvement

Patients continue practicing with updated exercises, track their growth, and work collaboratively with their therapist.
